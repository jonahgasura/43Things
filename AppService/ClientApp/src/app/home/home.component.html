What is 43 Things?

43 Things is the world's most popular online goal setting community. It's free and fun. How does it work?

List your Goals

Discover and list your goals. What do you want to do with your life? We'll help you figure it out.

Cheer

Cheer people on the side who inspire you. They may cheer you back!

Record your progress

Track your progress by writing entries. Ask questions and talk with people who share similar goals.

Get started

Make a practice list of goals or create an account (43 Things is free)
